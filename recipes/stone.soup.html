Stone Soup

Back to Odin Recipes

Hearthy delight

Have you ever been out for a walk, got lost in thought, and ended up as a medieval monk,
strolling hungry through a village of people who are hesitant to share their hard earned food with you, a complete stranger?
Than this is the perfect recipe (scam) for you!

Ingredients

Serving size: depends on how many people show up
  • 1 regular stone, preferably one that looks interesting (don't let anyone see you picking it up)
  • water from the nearest body of fresh water
  • diverse assortment of curious and gullible people

Utensils

  • 1 soup capable pot (fire resistent)

Steps

  1. Pick up your reliable soup pot and head to nearest fresh water source
  2. Fill the pot
  3. Head back to village center, and start a small cooking fire
    (make sure everyone in the village can see you!)
  4. Place the pot in the fire, and drop the stone inside the boiling water
  5. When the villagers become curious, tell them you're preparing a delicious stone soup,
    and you are more than willing to share the dish, but unfortunately you're missing just
    a couple of ingredients to make it really delightful
  6. Sit back and enjoy as the villagers run home and back to add their potatoes, carrots, garlic,
    olive oil, pieces of meat, and all the other tasty food to actually make the soup
  7. Regale with the locals
  8. Wash your trusty pot and stone, and save them for use in your next stop
